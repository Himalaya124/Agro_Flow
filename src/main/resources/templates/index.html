About AgroFlow

AgroFlow is a digital platform designed to help Krishi Officers manage farmer data, crop planning, harvesting, and sales submissions. With government supervision and field officer input, AgroFlow ensures transparency, efficiency, and fair pricing for farmers across Bangladesh.
